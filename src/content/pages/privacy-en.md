## 1. Information Collection
### 1.1 Information You Provide
When you use FemRes, we may collect the following information:
* Account information (username, email address)
* Profile information (nickname, avatar, bio)
* Content contributions (books, articles, videos, podcasts you recommend)
* Comments and feedback

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
Information we collect automatically includes:
* Browser type and version
* Operating system
* IP address (used only for geolocation statistics, full IP not stored)
* Access times and page browsing history
* Search history and content preferences

## 2. Information Usage
We use collected information to:
* Provide personalized content recommendations
* Improve website functionality and user experience
* Send relevant content updates (with your consent)
* Prevent abuse and maintain platform security
* Conduct anonymized data analysis and research

## 3. Information Sharing
We promise:
* Not to sell your personal information
* Not to share your information with third-party advertisers
* Only to disclose information when required by law or to protect user safety
* When sharing necessary information with trusted service providers (e.g., cloud storage services), ensure they adhere to equivalent privacy standards

## 4. Data Security
We take the following measures to protect your information:
* Use HTTPS to encrypt all data transmission
* Regularly conduct security audits and vulnerability scans
* Restrict employee access to user data
* Encrypt sensitive information
* Regularly back up data to prevent loss

## 5. Cookie Usage
We use Cookies to:
* Remember your login status
* Save your preferences (e.g., theme, language)
* Analyze website traffic and usage patterns

You can manage or disable Cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the use of certain functions.

## 6. Your Rights
Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:
* **Right of Access**: View the information we collect about you
* **Right to Rectification**: Update or correct inaccurate information
* **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal information
* **Right to Portability**: Obtain a copy of your data
* **Right to Object**: Object to specific data processing activities

## 7. Children's Privacy
FemRes is not directed to children under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will delete that information immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfer
Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers outside your country/region. We ensure these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 9. Policy Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you via the website or email. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 10. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us:
* Email: privacy@femres.org
* [Contact Us](/contact)
